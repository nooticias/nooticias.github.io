After Michael Sam got drafted by the St. Louis Rams in the seventh round of Saturday's NFL Draft — making him league's first openly gay draftee in its 94-year history — congratulations and adulations poured in from all corners of the Internet. Rams brass insisted they simply "drafted a good football player," with no mind toward his sexual orientation. U.S. President Barack Obama released a statement commending Sam's trailblazing status.
