Roger Goodell spent the better part of three decades climbing his way up the NFL ladder, starting as an intern in 1982 before being named the league's commissioner in 2006. But in the space of three days since TMZ released a video of Baltimore Ravens star Ray Rice decking his then-fiance (now wife) Janay Palmer out cold in a hotel elevator, Goodell's decades of true-blue company man loyalty may have disintegrated right through his fingers.
